Marcel Hemp

Who

We are located in the wonderful beachy town of Santa Barbara at 531 State Street and have been in this same location for 20 years! The face you see on all our clothing is a picture of Marcel when he was about 2 years old.

Why Our store was founded on the hope that we can provide for a more sustainable future for our community by bringing an earth-friendly clothing line to the Santa Barbara area. Unlike many other options in the market, our line of products is both ecologically and socially responsible, not to mention, all organically grown. We design and manufacture all of our own clothing and believe that fair trade practices are the only way to do business. Why not choose clothes that you can feel good about wearing?
